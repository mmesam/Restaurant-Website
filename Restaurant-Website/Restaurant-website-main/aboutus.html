About Us

Welcome to Johnson's Mix, an exquisite Jamaican Fusion restaurant that transcends culinary boundaries and invites you to indulge in a symphony of flavors. Nestled in north Oshawa, we pride ourselves on redefining traditional Jamaican cuisine by infusing it with global influences, creating a unique and unforgettable dining experience. Our diverse menu at Johnson's Mix is a celebration of fusion, blending the authentic taste of Jamaica with international culinary inspirations. From the explosive Jamaican Hot Pepper Shrimp to the comforting Chicken Alfredo with a Caribbean twist, each dish is a harmonious fusion that reflects the creativity and innovation of our culinary team.

Meet our culinary maestro, Master Chef Dajuan, whose expertise and passion for pushing culinary boundaries shape the distinctive flavors of Johnson's Mix. Chef Dajuan brings a wealth of experience and a bold approach to the kitchen, creating dishes that are a true celebration of fusion gastronomy. Guiding the vision of Johnson's Mix is our visionary CEO, Mesam. With a deep appreciation for diverse culinary experiences, Mesam leads our restaurant into new realms of flavor exploration. His commitment to excellence is evident in every fusion creation that graces our menu. Ensuring a seamless and delightful dining experience is our General Manager, Farzad. With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to hospitality, Farzad orchestrates the smooth operation of Johnson's Mix, making every visit a fusion of exceptional service and delectable cuisine.

At Johnson's Mix, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that transcends borders. Immerse yourself in the fusion of flavors, where traditional meets contemporary, and each bite tells a tale of creativity and passion. Join us as we redefine the art of dining at Johnson's Mix, where the world converges on your plate in an explosion of taste and innovation
